Today's Cone

The ice cream cone first shown at the World Fair remains the most popular way to eat Ice Cream, since it's quick and easy, making it perfect as a short dessert. You might see the famous ice cream cone sold in Chick-Fil-A, where it's the basic Vanilla Ice Cream with the cone! The cone helps makes it portable and lickable.

More Than Vanila?

Ice Cream has also expanded far beyond the standard vanila flavor. Chocolate, Strawberry, Peach, Coffee, MINT CHOCOLATE, Cookie Dough, Rocky Road... ice cream manafucaters have been thinking of new ice cream flavors beyond Vanilla. It's estimated over a thousnad different flavors of ice cream worldwide!
